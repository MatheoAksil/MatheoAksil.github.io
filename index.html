About me

I am a second-year PhD student based in Paris (France), interested in the interactions between theoretical physics and biology. My current work mainly focuses on the mathematical modeling of biological phenomena, from the molecular scale of the gene to the macroscopic description of developing organs and tissues. You can download my CV for further informations.
In my lab, I am also involved in a science outreach program that integrates both in-school and lab visits to engage students from underprivileged backgrounds. Learn more on this page.

Research interests

  • Stochastic processes and statistical field theories
  • Gene regulation and developmental programs
  • Instabilities and morphogenesis
  • Active matter
